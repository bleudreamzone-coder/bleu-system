## The thesis

**BLEU is the clinically governed lens between general AI and vulnerable wellness decisions.**

General-purpose AI is brilliant and everywhere. It will answer any health question instantly, fluently, and confidently — including the ones asked at 2am by someone scared, grieving, or spiraling. That fluency is not the same as safety. The gap between *a confident answer* and *a safe next step* is exactly where people get hurt.

BLEU lives in that gap. Not as another oracle, but as a lens: it takes the moment, runs it through clinical governance and safety routing, and returns the lowest-regret next step — then remembers what happened so the next step gets better.

> **Big LLMs remember the person. BLEU protects the decision.**

A general model optimizes to know you and keep you engaged. BLEU optimizes to protect the specific decision in front of you, especially when you are least able to protect it yourself.

## The three-voice stack

Every BLEU surface must hold three voices at once. If any one is missing, the surface is not BLEU.

1. **User voice — warmth.** Feel → validate → solve. The person is met as a person first. *"Arrive confused. Leave clearer."*
2. **Clinical voice — restraint.** Evidence tiers, claim boundaries, refusal, the 988 floor, Dr. Felicia Stoler DCN review. Never diagnose. Never overclaim.
3. **Infrastructure voice — durability.** Audit trail, idempotency, RLS, privacy hashing, fail-safes. The boring machinery that makes the warmth trustworthy.

Warmth without clinical restraint is dangerous. Clinical restraint without warmth is cold and unused. Both without infrastructure is theater.

## The user tagline

> **Arrive confused. Leave clearer.**

Not "leave cured." Not "leave sold." Clearer. That is the honest, deliverable promise.

## The sacred line

> **The future does not need more light. It needs a clinically governed lens for the moments when humans are most vulnerable.**

Light is abundant — every model is a floodlight. Lenses are rare. A lens does not add brightness; it focuses, filters, and protects. BLEU is the lens.
