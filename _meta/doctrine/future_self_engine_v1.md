## The movement, not the product

> **From wish to evidence.**

The Future Self Engine is the layer that turns a person's stated wish ("I want to sleep through the night," "I want my energy back") into a governed, evidence-tracked trajectory toward that future self — and shows them the distance closing with real data, not affirmations.

The product is a feature. The **movement** is the hidden thing underneath: a wellness culture that measures whether people actually got better, instead of whether they stayed engaged. BLEU's outcome loop (day-3 / day-7 / day-30 checks) is the seed of that movement. The Future Self Engine is where it becomes visible to the user.

---

## How it works (conceptual)

1. **Wish capture** — the user names a future self in their own words.
2. **Governed translation** — the wish becomes a bounded, evidence-tiered protocol (passes the seven gates in [[coca_cola_recipe_v1]]).
3. **Trajectory tracking** — outcome checks record movement toward (or away from) the wish.
4. **Honest mirror** — the user sees real progress, including when it stalls. Stalls are data, not failure.

---

## Governance boundaries (hard)

- **No cosmic claims.** This is not manifestation. The future self is reached by evidence-backed steps and measured outcomes, never by belief, frequency, or destiny. (Refusal 15.)
- **No blame.** If the trajectory stalls or reverses, that is information about the route, never a verdict on the person. (Refusal 16.)
- **No certainty beyond tier.** Projections are labeled by evidence tier and never promised. (Refusal 13.)
- **Clinician, not oracle.** The engine routes toward human care at every red flag; it never positions itself as the authority on someone's body. (Refusals 5, 6, 17.)
- **Founder excitement does not ship features.** Outcome data gates the build, not enthusiasm. (Refusal 19.)
