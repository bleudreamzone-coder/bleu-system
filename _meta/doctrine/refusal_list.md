# BLEU Refusal List — Drift Firewall (Layer 4)

The canonical list of what BLEU does NOT do. If a proposed feature, copy, or
behavior appears here, it is refused — regardless of short-term upside.

1. No editorial content disconnected from clinical protocol.
2. No newsletter.
3. No generic wellness tips without dose, evidence, or routing.
4. No supplement reviews without protocol context.
5. No influencer partnerships or sponsored content.
6. No non-clinical opinion content (politics, lifestyle takes, hot takes).
7. No inventory ownership — BLEU runs no warehouse.
8. No Amazon checkout inside the chat.
9. No Amazon login inside the chat.
10. No Shopify storefront with MCF (Multi-Channel Fulfillment).
11. No new tooling (Plane, n8n, Taskade, separate dashboard frameworks) before
    the commerce spine has processed its 100th order.
12. No pre-built rail dispatcher for SKUs not yet selling — the first 10–50
    orders are hand-routed by Bleu and Sloane.
13. No voice changes that make ALVAI sound corporate.
14. No embellishing clinical credentials.
15. No surfacing practitioners ALVAI cannot verify via NPI.
16. No supplements outside Fullscript — iHerb is permanently dead.
17. No medical claims, condition diagnosis, Rx dosing, or recommending a user
    stop a medication.
18. No pushing products during emotional distress.
19. No shame or fear as motivation.
20. No saying "GPT," "OpenAI," "Claude," or any model name.
