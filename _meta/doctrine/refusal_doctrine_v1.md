# Refusal Doctrine v1 — What BLEU Refuses To Do

**Status:** LOCKED. Inherits from [[source_document_v1]].
**Last reviewed:** 2026-05-26

A product is defined as much by what it refuses as by what it ships. These are not preferences; they are constraints with the force of architecture. Each one should be testable against the code and the copy. See also the operational [[refusal_list]] and [[pressure_architecture_v1]].

1. **Will not sell during crisis.** Crisis detection overrides commerce. The 988 path supersedes every funnel.
2. **Will not gamify wellness.** No streaks, points, or loss-aversion mechanics that manufacture compulsion.
3. **Will not employ dark patterns.** No confirmshaming, fake scarcity, hidden unsubscribes, or roach-motel flows.
4. **Will not let commerce override safety.** The commerce gate is downstream of the safety gate, always.
5. **Will not claim to diagnose.** Education and wellness support only; diagnosis belongs to a clinician.
6. **Will not replace clinicians.** BLEU routes *to* care; it does not stand in for it.
7. **Will not sell user data.** The business model is trust, not the resale of vulnerability.
8. **Will not use fear, urgency, or shame to convert.** Conversion built on a nervous-system hijack is a refund waiting to happen and a person harmed.
9. **Will not allow unsafe claims past clinical review.** Dr. Felicia Stoler DCN review is a gate, not a garnish.
10. **Will not downgrade the 988 banner for UX.** Aesthetics never trim the crisis floor.
11. **Will not optimize for engagement over trajectory.** Time-on-app is not the metric; the person getting better is.
12. **Will not isolate users from real people.** BLEU points toward humans, community, and practitioners — never away.
13. **Will not pretend certainty beyond evidence tier.** Established, emerging, experimental, narrative — each labeled honestly.
14. **Will not let a single reviewer become a single point of failure.** Clinical governance must survive any one person.
15. **Will not claim manifestation or magical thinking.** No cosmic guarantees. Authority is data.
16. **Will not blame users for outcomes.** Outcomes are data to learn from, never a verdict on the person.
17. **Will not use medical authority overreach.** Credentials inform; they do not bully.
18. **Will not inflate doctrine faster than functioning reality.** The document never gets ahead of the running system.
19. **Will not let founder excitement override outcome data.** The data leads; enthusiasm follows.
20. **Will not accept capital that demands growth over care.** Money that requires breaking 1–19 is the wrong money.
