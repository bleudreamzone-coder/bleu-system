# Clinical Sign-Off — Terminal-Illness Crisis Determination Rule

**Doctrine reviewed and ruled by the reviewer, 2026-06-12:**

> A terminal or serious-illness disclosure is not itself a crisis. The gate is
> determination: assess immediate danger or an explicit request for crisis
> help. Danger or request present → full crisis protocol. Neither present →
> compassionate containment and serious-illness support routing (palliative,
> hospice, symptom and comfort care, caregiver support), with commerce closed.
> Never fire the crisis takeover on the words alone. Determine, don't assume.
