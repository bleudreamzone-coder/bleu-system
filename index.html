TERPENE INTELLIGENCE

🥭 Myrcene
Sedating, anti-inflammatory. GABA-A agonist. Found in mangoes. Enhances THC absorption. Eat mango 45 min before.
🍋 Limonene
Mood elevation, anxiolytic. Boosts serotonin via 5-HT. Found in citrus. Anti-tumor in preclinical studies.
🌶 Caryophyllene
ONLY terpene that binds CB2 directly. A dietary cannabinoid. Anti-inflammatory. Chew peppercorn to calm THC anxiety.
🌲 Pinene
Alertness, memory. Counteracts THC memory loss via acetylcholinesterase inhibition. Forest-bathing molecule.
💜 Linalool
Sedative, anxiolytic, anticonvulsant. Modulates glutamate + GABA. Found in lavender. Sleep terpene.
🌸 Terpinolene
Uplifting, antioxidant. Only 10% of strains dominant. Creative spark. Found in lilac and tea tree.
